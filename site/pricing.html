Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about T-Minus pricing and plans.

Can I try Premium features before committing? ▾
Yes. Every Premium plan starts with a 14-day free trial -- no credit card required. You get full access to all Premium features. If you decide not to continue, your account downgrades to Free automatically.
What happens when I exceed my account limit? ▾
We will notify you and give you 30 days to upgrade or disconnect accounts. During that period, all your connected accounts continue to sync normally. We never cut you off without warning.
Is my calendar data encrypted? ▾
Yes. We use envelope encryption with per-user keys. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. Each user's data lives in an isolated Durable Object with its own SQLite database -- there is no shared table where your events mix with anyone else's.
Can I export my data? ▾
Full data portability is a core principle. You can export via CalDAV feeds, the REST API, or request a complete data archive. Enterprise plans include cryptographic deletion certificates that prove your data has been removed.
Do you sell my data? ▾
No. Never. Your temporal data is yours. We do not even have access to read it -- per-user Durable Object isolation means your calendar events are stored in a private database that only your authenticated requests can reach. There is no analytics pipeline, no data brokering, no ad targeting.
What's the MCP server? ▾
Model Context Protocol (MCP) lets AI assistants -- Claude, ChatGPT, and others -- interact with your calendar through natural language. T-Minus exposes 26 tools for reading events, scheduling meetings, checking availability, and managing relationships. Your AI assistant becomes calendar-aware without screen scraping or brittle integrations.
